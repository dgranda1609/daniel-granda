## CRITICAL BUGS — Articles Page (`pages/Articles.tsx`)

### Bug 1 — Wrong property: `item.published_at` / `item.curated_at`
**File:** `pages/Articles.tsx:50`
**Issue:** Component accesses `item.published_at` and `item.curated_at` which do NOT exist on the `FeedItem` type.
**FeedItem actual shape:** `{ date: string, type: 'post' | 'curated', slug_or_url: string, ... }`
**Fix:** Replace with `item.date`

### Bug 2 — Wrong type check: `item.type === 'link'`
**File:** `pages/Articles.tsx:54`
**Issue:** The `FeedItemType` union is `'post' | 'curated'` — `'link'` is not a valid value.
**Fix:** Change to `item.type === 'curated'`

### Bug 3 — Wrong property: `item.url`
**File:** `pages/Articles.tsx:55`
**Issue:** `FeedItem` has no `url` property — the URL for curated links is at `slug_or_url`.
**Fix:** Replace `item.url` with `item.slug_or_url`

### Bug 4 — Invalid imports: `BlogPost`, `CuratedLink` from `lib/api`
**File:** `pages/Articles.tsx:4`
**Issue:** `lib/api.ts` does NOT export `BlogPost` or `CuratedLink`. These types are unused since the page works with `FeedItem[]`.
**Fix:** Remove `BlogPost, CuratedLink` from imports.

### Bug 5 — Brand violation: `rounded-lg` on article cards
**File:** `pages/Articles.tsx:46`
**Issue:** Brand principle is 0px radius (sharp, editorial). `rounded-lg` adds 8px corner radius.
**Fix:** Remove `rounded-lg` class.
